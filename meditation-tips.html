Top 5 Meditation Tips for Beginners

Meditation can be a powerful tool to reduce stress and improve focus. Here are 5 tips to get started:

1. Start Small

Begin with just 1 minute a day, like our daily meditations, and gradually increase the time as you get comfortable.

2. Find a Quiet Space

Choose a calm environment where you won’t be disturbed. This helps you focus better.

3. Focus on Your Breath

Pay attention to your breathing. Inhale deeply and exhale slowly to relax your body.

4. Be Consistent

Try to meditate at the same time every day to build a habit.

5. Don’t Judge Yourself

It’s okay if your mind wanders. Gently bring your focus back without frustration.
